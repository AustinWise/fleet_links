How to make Fleet Links trusted

If you clicked "Yes" after checking the "Don't ask me about this site again" checkbox, EVE marks the site as permanatly untrsuted.
To fix this, click on the "Options" menu in the in game browser and select "Trusted Sites":

One there, right click on the "http://goldenenchantment.com/" entry and select "Make Trusted".

That should fix it.
